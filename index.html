The Story

Picky started because the founder Matias Dusil wanted a place to keep track of his ratings for the drinks he had ordered at Starbucks so that he could remember what he liked and didn't like.

How it Works

With Picky you can do two main things:

  • Choose the meal/drink that you ordered at the restaurant/cafe to give it a rating. That way you can always refer back to your list and check which products you liked most!
  • Look at your friends' favorite products so that you can try what they're having!
